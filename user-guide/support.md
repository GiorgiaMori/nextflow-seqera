Contact <seqera@biocommons.org.au> for support with the Australian Nextflow Seqera Service.

Include the following where relevant:

- your organisation and workspace name;
- a concise description of what you were trying to do;
- the date and approximate time of the problem;
- the workflow name and revision;
- the compute environment and infrastructure;
- the run URL or run identifier;
- the first relevant failed task and exit code;
- non-sensitive log excerpts or screenshots;
- steps already taken to troubleshoot the issue.

Do not send passwords, access tokens, private keys or sensitive research data by email.
