**For:** all users

## Log in

1. Go to [https://seqera.services.biocommons.org.au](https://seqera.services.biocommons.org.au).
2. Enter your email address.
3. Open the access email sent to your inbox and follow the sign-in link.
4. Return to the service if the sign-in flow opens in another browser tab.

<div style="text-align:center"><img width="50%" src="../assets/doc_img/login.png" alt="Australian Nextflow Seqera Service login screen"/></div>

## Switch workspaces

Use the workspace menu at the top left of the page to move between your personal workspace and any organisation workspaces you have joined.

If an expected workspace is missing, confirm that the workspace administrator added the same email address you used to log in.
