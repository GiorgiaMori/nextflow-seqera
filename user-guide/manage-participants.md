**For:** workspace owners and administrators  
**Required role:** workspace administrator or owner

## Add a participant

1. Ask the user to log in to the service once and confirm the email address they used.
2. Open the relevant workspace.
3. Open **Participants**.
4. Add the user's email address.
5. Assign the least-privileged role that allows the user to complete their work.

Add users to the workspace rather than the organisation unless organisation-level membership is specifically required.

## Choose an appropriate role

Use administrator or owner permissions only for people who need to manage participants, credentials, compute environments or other shared resources. See the [Seqera workspace administration documentation](https://docs.seqera.io/platform/latest/administration/overview#workspace-administration) for current role definitions.

## Remove access

Remove participants who no longer require access. Review membership periodically, particularly for workspaces containing shared credentials, restricted data or chargeable compute resources.
