**For:** all users  
**Required role:** workspace administrator only when adding other participants

## Join an existing organisation workspace

1. [Log in to the service](/user-guide/log-in) using the email address you want associated with your account.
2. Share that email address with the workspace administrator.
3. Ask the administrator to add you under the workspace **Participants** tab, rather than at the organisation level.
4. After you have been added, refresh the service and select the workspace from the workspace menu.

See the [Seqera membership administration documentation](https://docs.seqera.io/platform/latest/administration/overview#membership-administration) for general information about roles.

## Request a new organisation workspace

Contact <seqera@biocommons.org.au> and provide:

- the name and purpose of the proposed workspace;
- the organisation or research group responsible for it;
- the proposed workspace administrator;
- the expected users and workflows;
- the intended compute infrastructure.

Make sure you are familiar with the [workspace requirements](/nextflow-seqera/main/access-models).

## Access a personal workspace

Each user may see a personal workspace after logging in. Personal workspaces are not currently supported by the Australian BioCommons service team. Users are responsible for configuring and managing any resources used in a personal workspace.
